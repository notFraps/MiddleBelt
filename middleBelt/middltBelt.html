About Us

The concept of our cultural and natural heritage is based on historically changing and diverse value systems. These values are recognized by different groups of people within our region. The ideas developed and accepted by these different groups create various categories of cultural and natural heritage.

Our Mission

We aim to offer a bridge between the past and the future with the application of a particular approach in the present. Due to our attached values for these groups or societies, cultural heritage should be maintained in the present and bestowed for the benefit of future generations.
